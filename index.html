    more about the experience

    Duration: 1hr

    Everyday 7-8am

    Starts from Rialto Bridge

    Breakfast
    Included

    Tour Difficulty

    Easy – Moderate
    2-6 miles (you decide)

    During the experience, we will visit Venice's main attractions and promenades. You will have the opportunity to take in the views of the tranquil morning city, soak in the rhythm of the city, and perhaps feel like a Venetian out for a morning run. We will also take numerous photos to capture the beauty of Venice for you to cherish.

    Nice to meet you!

    My name is Denis. I have been a resident of Venice for over 14 years, and as an avid runner, I am thrilled to offer you an unforgettable experience of a serene morning city center, along with showcasing the main water-based attractions of the city. An added bonus for you is the opportunity to start your day with a refreshing workout and good vibes, which will surely accompany you throughout the rest of the day.
